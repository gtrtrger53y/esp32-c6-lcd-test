ESP32‑C6 LCD Test Firmware

Click the button below to install the test firmware onto your Waveshare ESP32‑C6‑LCD‑1.47 board. The firmware will exercise the display, RGB LED and Wi‑Fi scan capabilities. You must use a compatible browser such as Chrome or Edge on a desktop computer to access the USB serial port.

After flashing, open a serial monitor at 115200 baud to view diagnostic output. The on‑screen information will update on startup.
